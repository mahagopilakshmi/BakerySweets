About Aruvi Bakery & Sweets

Aruvi Bakery is Located in Perumal Puram, Palayankottai in Tirunelveli, it is an ancient city and is more than 2000 years old. The city is located on the west bank of the Thamirabarani River. In these days of mass production, it is rare to find a bakery that devotes itself to quality over quality. Aruvi Bakery uses only quality ingredients. All of our cakes are made from scratch with the finest ingredients, providing you with cakes of higher quality. Aruvi Bakery unique creations including Traditional tiered cakes, fresh cream cakes, wedding cakes, birthday cakes, Bread, Biscuits, sweets and snacks. Every Aruvi Bakery Products are produce from the Heart and its shows the taste as good as it looks.
